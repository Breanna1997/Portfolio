A Little About me

Hi, I'm Breanna Brownlee before programming I was a Sophmore majoring in Education. I have a passion for growing the minds of children. As I was preparing to take my last class to receive my Associats degree in Education but during that time COVID had just been annouced and I was not able to take my last class due to social distancing. When this huge unexpecte change happen I realized that being a educator wasn't the work life that I actually enjoy. My husband began to encourage me to look into the Tech industry so I did. I began Teaching myself HTML CSS and JavaScripts using books by Murachs. I fell inlove with Watching what I created come life. Even the smallest thing like Hello World! excited me.


After a year of being self taught I decided to enroll into a STLCC to obtain an Associate Degree in Software Development. During my time at STLCC I've taken courses such as Web Technologies where I learned more about HTML CSS and JavaScript, Web Scripting that taught more about JavaScript and JQuery, C# I course where I learned the fundamentals of object programming using C#, and Graphic Design course where I learned how to use Adobe Photoshop and Canva. Outside of college I'm also enrolled in a UX Designer Google Certification course through Digital Strike a local digital marketing company.
